Akame ga kill

Akame ga Kill! is a Japanese manga series created by Takahiro and illustrated by Tetsuya Tashiro. It started serialization in Square Enix's Gangan Joker in March 2010. The story focuses on Tatsumi, a young villager who travels to the Capital to raise money for his home only to discover strong corruption in the area.
Episodes: 24 (List of episodes)
English network: NA Anime NetworkUS Adult Swim (Toonami)

Genres: Adventure fiction, Dark fantasy
